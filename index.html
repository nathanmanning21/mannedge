Landing page

A landing page is a standalone responsive webpage which consists of one website page. A high quality landing page can grab customers attention with a navigation bar which will link them down to the specific page of the website.

Overview

1. Header section which will house your logo, navigation and contact details. When a customer clicks on a navigation link the website will scroll to the specific part of the landing page that has been directed

2. Image banner, where your customers will get a big high quality image of what your business can provide and the services you offer

3. About section, this section includes a brief summary about your company, its history and any important details such as awards etc.

4. Services section is where you can display an overview of services that you provide

5. Contact section will contain important contact details, a ‘contact us form’ and google map if you require

6. Footer section, this is the final section of your landing page, where details such as social media links are kept.
